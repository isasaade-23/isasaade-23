## About &middot; Sobre

**EN** &mdash; I build bridges between **public health** and **machine learning**, from one
conviction: technology is most powerful when it serves collective needs. I apply LLMs,
knowledge graphs and predictive models to real health data &mdash; clinical text in
Portuguese, patient flow across Brazil's public health system (SUS), and longitudinal cohorts.

**PT** &mdash; Construo pontes entre **saúde pública** e **machine learning**, partindo de uma
convicção: a tecnologia é mais poderosa quando serve a necessidades coletivas. Aplico LLMs,
grafos de conhecimento e modelos preditivos a dados reais de saúde &mdash; texto clínico em
português, fluxo assistencial no SUS e coortes longitudinais.

## Current focus &middot; Foco atual

- **LLMs & GraphRAG** &mdash; reconstruct patient journeys from health data
- **NLP in Portuguese** &mdash; clinical information extraction
- Efficient **fine-tuning** (LoRA/QLoRA) and rigorous **model evaluation**
- **MLOps** &mdash; reproducible pipelines, from raw data to served model

## Featured projects &middot; Projetos em destaque

**[regionalizacao-sus-graphrag](https://github.com/isasaade-23/regionalizacao-sus-graphrag)** &mdash; GraphRAG over patient journeys in Brazil's public health system
> Knowledge graph in **Neo4j** plus **natural-language-to-Cypher** via LLM, using breast cancer
> as a tracer condition. QLoRA fine-tuning and multi-model evaluation by execution.
>
> `Python` &middot; `Neo4j` &middot; `GraphRAG` &middot; `LLM` &middot; `QLoRA` &middot; `NLP-PT`

**[who-zscore-python](https://github.com/isasaade-23/who-zscore-python)** &mdash; WHO growth reference z-scores
> BMI-for-age and height-for-age z-scores (5&ndash;19 years) via the LMS method, validated
> against the official WHO `anthroplus` package. Clean, vectorized code.
>
> `Python` &middot; `pandas` &middot; `numpy`
